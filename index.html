Features

My Journey Dashboard

See your whole story at a glance. Your dashboard brings together everything that matters—current weight, lbs lost, days active, weigh-in count, and your progress toward your summit goal—in one beautiful, motivating view.

  • Weight trend charts from 1 week to all time
  • Milestone badges you can share (down 5, 10, 25 lbs and more)
  • Hero cards for every metric: weight, hydration, therapy, fasting
  • Journey Points and Trail Level gamification to keep you motivated
💉

GLP‑1 Therapy Tracking

Journey Tracker was built alongside the GLP‑1 experience. Log every injection or oral dose, track dose escalations over time, and see how your weight responds as your therapy progresses.

  • Log medication name, dose, injection site, and notes
  • Dose timeline and weight-vs-dose correlation charts
  • Recurring reminders so you never miss a dose
  • Supports Zepbound, Ozempic, Wegovy, and more
💧

Fasting & Hydration

Staying on top of hydration and fasting is essential on any GLP‑1 journey. Track your daily water intake against personal goals, monitor fasting windows, and celebrate consistency streaks.

  • Daily water log with intake history and goal tracking
  • Fasting window start/end with streak milestones
  • Calendar activity view across all your logged days
  • Syncs water intake to Apple Health
🔒

Private by Design

Your health data belongs to you—and only you. Journey Tracker is built from the ground up around Apple's privacy infrastructure. We collect no advertising identifiers and operate no servers that store your health data.

  • Sign in with Apple keeps your identity private
  • Face ID / Touch ID app lock for physical security
  • Data stored in your personal iCloud via CloudKit—not our servers
  • No ads, no analytics SDKs, no data sales—ever

Privacy Policy

Your health data stays yours.

Journey Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. We are not a healthcare provider, health plan, or healthcare clearinghouse. We collect only the information necessary to operate the app's features, and we never sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information to any third party for any purpose, including advertising.

Information You Provide Directly
  • Body measurements: Weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass, BMI, and related metrics
  • Medication information: GLP-1 medication name, dose, injection dates, and injection site rotation
  • Side effect logs: Symptoms experienced during therapy, severity, and associated notes
  • Laboratory results: HbA1c, lipid panel, metabolic markers, thyroid markers, and other health markers you choose to enter
  • Health goals: Target weight and hydration goals
  • Notes: Free-text notes you add to any health entry
Information Collected Through Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, Journey Tracker may read from and write to Apple HealthKit. Data types accessed may include:

  • Body weight and body composition metrics
  • Blood glucose readings
  • Blood pressure measurements
  • Dietary water intake
  • Laboratory results, if you connect your health system through Apple Health Records

You control HealthKit access entirely. Revoke any permission at any time: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Journey Tracker.

Information We Do NOT Collect
  • Your full name
  • Your physical address or precise location
  • Your date of birth
  • Your Social Security Number or government-issued identifiers
  • Your health insurance information
  • Your prescribing physician's information
  • Payment card information — payments are processed entirely by Apple's App Store

Sign in with Apple provides us only with a stable, opaque user identifier — not your Apple ID or real name.

How We Store Your Information
📱

On Your Device

All health data is stored locally using Apple's Core Data framework with AES-256 encryption via iOS FileProtection. Your data is accessible only when your device is unlocked.

☁️

Your iCloud Account

If you have iCloud enabled, data syncs to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework — not any server controlled by us. Journey Tracker developers cannot access your data under any circumstances. You can disable iCloud backup at any time: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud.

AI-Powered Lab Analysis (Pro Feature)

If you subscribe to Journey Tracker Pro and choose to use the AI Lab Analysis feature, a de-identified version of your laboratory results and medication information is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude AI. Before this feature is activated, you will be shown a separate consent screen explaining exactly what data is transmitted.

✅

What Is Transmitted to the AI

Your lab values as numbers (e.g., "HbA1c: 6.2") · GLP-1 medication name and current dose · Relative therapy duration (e.g., "Week 14") · Relative changes in your values over time

🚫

What Is NEVER Transmitted

Your name or any identifier · Exact dates or timestamps · Your Apple ID or account information · Any information that could identify you as an individual

Analysis results are cached on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not retain a copy on any server we control. You can disable AI Lab Analysis at any time: Settings → Privacy & Security → AI Lab Analysis.

Information Sharing
  • Apple: We use Apple's platform services (HealthKit, CloudKit, Sign in with Apple, StoreKit). Your use of these services is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy (apple.com/privacy).
  • Anthropic (AI Analysis feature only): If you consent, de-identified health data is transmitted to Anthropic, PBC for processing. Data is de-identified as described above and does not include any information that identifies you. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat (subscription management): Subscription status information is shared with RevenueCat to verify your Pro subscription. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • No other third-party sharing. We do not share your health or personal information with any other parties.
Your Rights and Choices

Access & Delete Your Data

  • View all data within the Journey Tracker app at any time
  • Delete individual entries within the app
  • Use Settings → Data → Clear All Data to remove all on-device data
  • Delete the app to remove all on-device data
  • Manage iCloud backups: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage

State Privacy Rights

  • California (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know, delete, opt out of sale (we do not sell data), and non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
  • Virginia (CDPA), Colorado (CPA), and other states: Similar rights to access, delete, and correct your personal information
  • To exercise these rights, contact us at support@journeytracker.app with the subject line "Privacy Rights Request"
Data Security
  • On-device encryption: All health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure authentication: Sign in with Apple with optional biometric lock (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Secure transmission: All data transmitted over the internet uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • De-identification: Health data transmitted for AI analysis is de-identified before transmission
  • No developer server access: We do not operate servers that store your health data

No security system is perfect. In the event of a security breach affecting your health information, we will notify affected users in accordance with the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule and applicable state law.

Children's Privacy

Journey Tracker is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child under 18 has provided health information through Journey Tracker, please contact us at support@journeytracker.app and we will take steps to delete that information.
