Features

A calm, focused companion for the long trail ahead—beautifully designed for Apple platforms.

🧪

Lab Results Tracking

Keep every blood test result in one place and watch your biomarkers improve over time. Log HbA1c, cholesterol, metabolic panels, thyroid markers, and more—with a beautiful calendar view that puts your lab history at your fingertips.

  • Track HbA1c, LDL, total cholesterol, and 20+ biomarkers
  • Calendar view shows exactly when each test was taken
  • Trend charts reveal how labs improve as therapy progresses
  • AI-powered lab analysis available with Pro subscription
🌡️

Trail Conditions (Symptoms)

GLP-1 therapy comes with a learning curve. Track every symptom—nausea, fatigue, and more—with severity ratings so you can see patterns, share accurate reports with your doctor, and understand how your body responds to each dose change.

  • Log symptoms with mild, moderate, or severe ratings
  • Automatically linked to the dose that preceded each symptom
  • Timeline view shows which symptoms resolve as therapy continues
  • Exportable summary for your next provider appointment
🗺️

My Journey Dashboard

See your whole story at a glance. Your dashboard brings together everything that matters—current weight, lbs lost, days active, weigh-in count, and your progress toward your summit goal—in one beautiful, motivating view.

  • Weight trend charts from 1 week to all time
  • Milestone badges you can share (down 5, 10, 25 lbs and more)
  • Hero cards for every metric: weight, hydration, therapy, fasting
  • Journey Points and Trail Level gamification to keep you motivated
💉

GLP‑1 Therapy Tracking

Journey Tracker was built alongside the GLP‑1 experience. Log every injection or oral dose, track dose escalations over time, and see how your weight responds as your therapy progresses.

  • Log medication name, dose, injection site, and notes
  • Dose timeline and weight-vs-dose correlation charts
  • Recurring reminders so you never miss a dose
  • Supports Zepbound, Ozempic, Wegovy, and more
💧

Fasting & Hydration

Staying on top of hydration and fasting is essential on any GLP‑1 journey. Track your daily water intake against personal goals, monitor fasting windows, and celebrate consistency streaks.

  • Daily water log with intake history and goal tracking
  • Fasting window start/end with streak milestones
  • Calendar activity view across all your logged days
  • Syncs water intake to Apple Health
🔒

Private by Design

Your health data belongs to you—and only you. Journey Tracker is built from the ground up around Apple's privacy infrastructure. We collect no advertising identifiers and operate no servers that store your health data.

  • Sign in with Apple keeps your identity private
  • Face ID / Touch ID app lock for physical security
  • Data stored in your personal iCloud via CloudKit—not our servers
  • No ads, no analytics SDKs, no data sales—ever
